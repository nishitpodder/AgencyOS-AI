# Lead Analysis Prompt

---

# Objective

Analyze a client's project requirements and determine whether the lead is worth pursuing.

The analysis should help agencies reduce manual work while improving decision-making.

---

# Input

The AI will receive:

- Client Name
- Company Name
- Industry
- Target Audience
- Product / Service
- Required Features
- Budget
- Timeline
- Project Description
- Additional Notes

---

# AI Analysis Steps

## Step 1 – Understand the Business

Identify:

- Business Type
- Industry
- Target Audience
- Primary Business Goal

---

## Step 2 – Understand the Project

Identify:

- Website
- Mobile App
- Web Application
- AI Automation
- CRM
- SaaS
- E-commerce
- Other

---

## Step 3 – Analyze Features

Evaluate:

- Number of Features
- Feature Complexity
- Third-party Integrations
- Payment Gateway
- Authentication
- Admin Panel
- Reports
- AI Features

Classify Complexity:

- Low
- Medium
- High
- Enterprise

---

## Step 4 – Budget Analysis

Determine whether the budget is:

- Too Low
- Acceptable
- Good
- Excellent

Explain your reasoning.

---

## Step 5 – Timeline Analysis

Evaluate whether the requested delivery timeline is:

- Realistic
- Challenging
- Unrealistic

Provide reasons.

---

## Step 6 – Requirement Clarity

Rate requirement clarity:

0–100%

Mention any missing information.

---

## Step 7 – Risk Analysis

Identify risks such as:

- Budget Risk
- Timeline Risk
- Requirement Risk
- Scope Creep
- Communication Risk

---

## Step 8 – Opportunity Analysis

Evaluate:

- Revenue Potential
- Long-term Client Value
- Upsell Opportunities
- Referral Potential

---

## Step 9 – Lead Score

Generate a score from 0–100.

Categories:

80–100 → Hot Lead 🔴

50–79 → Warm Lead 🟠

0–49 → Cold Lead 🔵

Explain the score.

---

## Step 10 – Recommendation

Provide one recommendation:

- Proceed Immediately
- Request More Information
- Negotiate Budget
- Decline Politely

Explain why.

---

# Output Format

## Lead Summary

## Business Analysis

## Project Analysis

## Budget Analysis

## Timeline Analysis

## Requirement Clarity

## Risk Analysis

## Opportunity Analysis

## Lead Score

## Final Recommendation

## Confidence Level (%)
