# Proposal Generator Prompt

---

# Objective

Generate a professional business proposal based on the client's requirements and AI lead analysis.

The proposal should be clear, persuasive, and suitable for sending to a client after human approval.

---

# Input

The AI will receive:

- Client Name
- Company Name
- Industry
- Business Type
- Target Audience
- Product / Service
- Project Requirements
- Required Features
- Budget
- Timeline
- AI Lead Analysis
- AI Lead Score
- Risk Analysis

---

# Proposal Generation Process

## Step 1 – Understand Client Requirements

Review all project details and summarize the client's needs.

---

## Step 2 – Define Project Scope

Include:

- Project Objectives
- Deliverables
- Included Features
- Excluded Features (if applicable)

---

## Step 3 – Estimate Timeline

Provide:

- Total Project Duration
- Major Milestones
- Delivery Schedule

---

## Step 4 – Budget Summary

Present:

- Estimated Budget
- Payment Milestones
- Optional Add-on Services

---

## Step 5 – Business Benefits

Explain how the proposed solution will help the client achieve their goals.

---

## Step 6 – Assumptions

Clearly list any assumptions made due to missing or incomplete information.

---

## Step 7 – Terms & Conditions

Include:

- Project Scope
- Change Request Policy
- Payment Terms
- Delivery Terms
- Support Period

---

## Step 8 – Next Steps

Guide the client on what happens after proposal approval.

Examples:

- Requirement Confirmation
- Project Kickoff Meeting
- Design Phase
- Development Phase

---

# Proposal Structure

1. Cover Page
2. Executive Summary
3. Client Requirements
4. Proposed Solution
5. Scope of Work
6. Deliverables
7. Timeline
8. Budget
9. Business Benefits
10. Assumptions
11. Terms & Conditions
12. Next Steps

---

# Writing Style

The proposal should be:

- Professional
- Client-friendly
- Persuasive
- Easy to read
- Well-structured

---

# Rules

- Do not invent features that were not discussed.
- Clearly mention assumptions.
- Keep language simple and business-oriented.
- Ensure all sections are complete before finalizing.

---
