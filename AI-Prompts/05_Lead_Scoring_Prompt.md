# Lead Scoring Prompt

---

# Objective

Evaluate the quality of a lead and assign a lead score between 0 and 100.

The score should help agencies prioritize high-value opportunities and avoid spending time on poor-quality leads.

---

# Input

The AI will receive:

- Client Name
- Company Name
- Industry
- Target Audience
- Product / Service
- Required Features
- Budget
- Timeline
- Project Description
- AI Analysis Results

---

# Scoring Criteria

## Budget (25 Points)

Evaluate:

- Very Low
- Low
- Medium
- High
- Excellent

Explain why.

---

## Requirement Clarity (20 Points)

Evaluate:

- Clear
- Partial
- Unclear

Identify missing information.

---

## Timeline (15 Points)

Evaluate:

- Realistic
- Challenging
- Unrealistic

---

## Business Potential (15 Points)

Evaluate:

- Long-term Opportunity
- Repeat Business
- Referral Potential

---

## Project Complexity (10 Points)

Evaluate whether the project complexity matches the agency's capabilities.

---

## Communication Quality (10 Points)

Evaluate:

- Client responsiveness
- Requirement quality
- Business understanding

---

## Strategic Value (5 Points)

Evaluate:

- Brand value
- Portfolio value
- Future partnership opportunities

---

# Final Lead Score

Generate a score between 0 and 100.

Categories

80–100 → Hot Lead 🔴

50–79 → Warm Lead 🟠

0–49 → Cold Lead 🔵

---

# Recommendation

Based on the score, recommend one of the following:

- Proceed Immediately
- Gather More Information
- Negotiate Budget
- Politely Decline

Explain your recommendation.

---

# Output Format

## Lead Score

## Score Breakdown

## Strengths

## Weaknesses

## Risks

## Recommendation

## Confidence Level
