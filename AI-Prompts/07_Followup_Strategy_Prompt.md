# Follow-up Strategy Prompt

---

# Objective

Generate an intelligent follow-up strategy after a proposal has been sent.

The AI should recommend the best next action based on the client's engagement and proposal status.

---

# Purpose

Help agencies maintain professional communication without missing opportunities or overwhelming clients.

---

# Input

The AI will receive:

- Client Name
- Company Name
- Proposal Status
- Proposal Sent Date
- Last Communication Date
- Client Response
- Lead Score
- Risk Level

---

# Follow-up Scenarios

## Scenario 1 – No Response

Recommend:

- Send a polite reminder after 3–5 business days.
- Summarize the proposal briefly.
- Ask if any clarification is needed.

---

## Scenario 2 – Client Requests Changes

Recommend:

- Update the proposal.
- Record requested changes.
- Generate a revised proposal version.

---

## Scenario 3 – Budget Concerns

Recommend:

- Suggest a phased implementation.
- Offer optional feature reductions.
- Explain value versus cost.

---

## Scenario 4 – Timeline Concerns

Recommend:

- Discuss realistic delivery milestones.
- Offer Phase 1 and Phase 2 planning.

---

## Scenario 5 – Proposal Approved

Recommend:

- Send a thank-you email.
- Schedule the kickoff meeting.
- Prepare onboarding documents.

---

# AI Recommendations

The AI may recommend:

- Send Reminder
- Schedule Meeting
- Revise Proposal
- Request Clarification
- Close Lead
- Escalate to Sales Manager

---

# Output Format

## Current Status

## Client Engagement

## Recommended Action

## Suggested Follow-up Email Summary

## Best Time to Contact

## Confidence Level (%)

---

# Rules

- Never pressure the client.
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone.
- Recommend actions based on the latest client activity.
- Avoid excessive follow-up emails.
