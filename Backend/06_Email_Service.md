# Email Service

---

# Purpose

This document describes how AgencyOS AI generates, approves, and sends emails to clients.

---

# Supported Email Types

- Proposal Email
- Follow-up Email
- Meeting Request
- Reminder Email
- Thank You Email

---

# Email Workflow

Lead Approved

↓

Generate Email

↓

Human Review

↓

Approve

↓

Send Email

↓

Log Activity

---

# Email Providers

Supported:

- SMTP
- Gmail
- Microsoft Outlook

Future:

- SendGrid
- Mailgun
- Amazon SES

---

# Email Components

Each email includes:

- Subject
- Greeting
- Personalized Body
- Call to Action
- Signature
- Company Branding

---

# Rules

- Human approval required before sending.
- Never invent pricing or project scope.
- Keep a copy of every sent email.
- Track email status.
