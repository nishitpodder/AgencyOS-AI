# User Stories

---

## US-001: User Registration

**As a** new agency owner,

**I want** to create an account,

**So that** I can securely access AgencyOS AI.

---

## US-002: User Login

**As a** registered user,

**I want** to log into the platform,

**So that** I can manage my business leads.

---

## US-003: Add New Lead

**As a** freelancer or agency owner,

**I want** to add a new client lead,

**So that** AI can analyze the project.

---

## US-004: AI Lead Analysis

**As a** business owner,

**I want** AI to understand client requirements,

**So that** I can save time analyzing every lead manually.

---

## US-005: AI Lead Score

**As a** business owner,

**I want** AI to calculate a lead score,

**So that** I can prioritize valuable leads.

---

## US-006: Proposal Generator

**As a** business owner,

**I want** AI to generate a professional proposal,

**So that** I can send proposals much faster.

---

## US-007: Human Approval

**As a** business owner,

**I want** to review AI-generated proposals and emails,

**So that** I can ensure all information is accurate before sending.

---

## US-008: Follow-up Email

**As a** business owner,

**I want** AI to generate a professional follow-up email,

**So that** I can improve client communication.

---

## US-009: Dashboard

**As a** business owner,

**I want** to see all my leads and AI activities in one place,

**So that** I can monitor my business efficiently.

---

## US-010: Lead History

**As a** business owner,

**I want** to view previous lead analyses and proposals,

**So that** I can track project history.
