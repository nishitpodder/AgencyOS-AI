# Project Overview

## Project Name

AgencyOS AI

---

## Project Type

AI-Powered SaaS Business Automation Platform

---

## MVP Module

LeadPilot AI – AI Lead Qualification & Proposal Generator

---

## Project Description

AgencyOS AI is an AI-powered SaaS platform designed to automate repetitive business operations for freelancers, digital agencies, marketing companies, and web development firms.

The first MVP module, LeadPilot AI, helps businesses analyze incoming leads, understand client requirements, calculate lead scores, generate project proposals, and prepare follow-up emails in just a few minutes.

The platform aims to reduce repetitive manual work, improve response speed, and increase business productivity through AI-driven automation.

---

## Problem Statement

Freelancers and digital agencies spend significant time on repetitive tasks after receiving a new lead.

These tasks include:

- Lead qualification
- Requirement analysis
- Budget estimation
- Timeline estimation
- Proposal generation
- Follow-up email creation

On average, these activities take around **30 minutes** per lead.

---

## Proposed Solution

AgencyOS AI automates the entire workflow using Artificial Intelligence.

The AI can:

- Analyze lead quality
- Understand project requirements
- Estimate project complexity
- Generate a lead score
- Create a professional proposal
- Draft a follow-up email

This reduces the processing time from **30 minutes to approximately 2–3 minutes** while keeping a human approval step before sending the proposal.
