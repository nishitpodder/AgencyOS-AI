# 🚀 AgencyOS AI

> AI Business Automation Platform for Digital Agencies

---

## 📌 Project Overview

AgencyOS AI is an AI-powered SaaS platform that automates repetitive business tasks for freelancers, digital agencies, and web development companies.

The MVP (Minimum Viable Product) focuses on **LeadPilot AI**, which helps agencies analyze leads, calculate lead scores, generate proposals, and prepare follow-up emails within a few minutes instead of spending 30+ minutes manually.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Freelancers and agencies spend a significant amount of time on repetitive tasks such as:

- Lead qualification
- Understanding client requirements
- Budget estimation
- Proposal creation
- Follow-up emails

These manual processes reduce productivity and slow down client response time.

---

## 💡 Solution

AgencyOS AI automates these tasks using Artificial Intelligence.

The MVP includes:

- AI Lead Analysis
- AI Lead Scoring
- AI Proposal Generator
- AI Follow-up Email Generator
- Human Review (HITL)
- Dashboard

---

## 🚀 Vision

Reduce repetitive agency work from **30 minutes to less than 3 minutes** using Artificial Intelligence.
