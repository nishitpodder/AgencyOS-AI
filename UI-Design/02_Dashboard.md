# Dashboard Design

## Purpose

The dashboard provides a quick overview of all business activities so users can immediately understand the status of their leads and AI automation.

---

# Dashboard Sections

## 1. Summary Cards

- Total Leads
- Hot Leads
- Pending Proposals
- Today's Tasks

---

## 2. Today's New Leads

Display all new leads received today.

Information:

- Client Name
- Company
- Budget
- Status

---

## 3. AI Notifications

Display AI processing updates.

Examples:

- Lead Analysis Complete
- Proposal Generated
- Email Draft Ready

---

## 4. Pending Approval

Display proposals waiting for human approval before sending.

Information:

- Proposal ID
- Client Name
- Date
- Status

---

## 5. Recent Activity

Display latest system activities.

Examples:

- Lead Added
- AI Analysis
- Proposal Generated
- Email Created
